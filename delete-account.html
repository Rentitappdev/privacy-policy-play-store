Account Deletion Process for Rent.It - seamless lease application

There are two ways to delete your account from Rent.It - seamless lease application:

Solution 1. Via sending e-mail message:
  1. Send an email to hello@rent-it.app from the email address you used to register in the application with the message stating your intention to delete your account.
Solution 2. Via application:
  1. Open Rent.It - seamless lease application application.
  2. Login to the account you want to delete.
  3. Go to your profile details (located at the right bottom corner of the screen, click on the icon with your profile photo).
  4. Select the "Login & Security" section.
  5. Press the "Delete Your Account" button at the bottom of the page.

By deleting your account, all personal details associated with the account will be removed from the system, including your name, phone number, country, city, and device IDs used for notification purposes.
